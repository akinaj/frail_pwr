Summary of interactive objects

1. arena_a

1. Exploding barrel.

Exploding barrel is a variation of conventional, metal barrel, which stores flammable liquid of type A. Thanks to its properties, exploding barrel can (and should) be used as a weapon - note that it's perfectly suited for traps. Explosive barrels come in variations with different radiuses: 2, 4 or 8 meters. When exploded, barrels inflict fire damage ranging from 5 to 15 points of life.

Exploding barrels uses following presets: barrel_e_r4, barrel_e_r2 or barrel_e_r8. Each of this presets indicates specific barrel with different radius. They implement barrel_a.lua script, responsible for spawning ribbons for barrels (these, in turn, indicate radius of a spawned barrel), deleting said ribbons when a barrel explodes and dealing damage to all actors within its radius.

2. Lava Lake.

This object occupies a circular area on the map of a given radius, ranging from 2 to 4 meters. If standing in its area of effect, actors take damage equal to 5 points of life per second.

Lava lakes uses following presets: lava_lake_r4, lava_lake_r2. Each of this presets indicates specific lava lake with different radius. They implement lava_lake_a.lua script, responsible for dealing damage to actors inside lake, increasing lava lake damage (if certain event happens) and spawning icons for actors indicatating they are, in fact, burning.

3. Invisibility bush.

This object occupies a circular area on the map of given radius, ranging from 1 to 2 meters. Bushes hide every actor in range. Actors inside the bush are invisible to actors outside of bush, but remain visible to other actors being inside bush (duh!).

Invisibility bushes uses following presets: invisiblity_bush_r2, invisiblity_bush_r1. Each of this presets indicates specific bush with a different radius. They implement invisibility_bush.lua script, which is responsible for changing the actors' visibility.

4. Power Lake.

This object occupies a circular area on the map of a given radius, ranging from 2 to 8 meters. Power lakes, in opposite to their lava counterparts, heal actors. Standing in the lake, actor has his life points restored by 5 per second.

Power lakes uses following presets: power_lake_r2, power_lake_r4, power_lake_r8. Each of this presets indicates specific power lake with a different radius. They implement power_lake.lua, which is responsible for healing actors inside the lake and spawning icon for actors indicatacing that they are being healed.

5. Stunning barrel.

Stunning barrel is a variation of exploding barrel storing flammable liquid of type B. Stunning barrels come with different radiuses, ranging from 2 to 8 meters. When exploded, barrel inflicts fire damage, taking 5 health points from actor and reducing his movement speed for a short time

Stunning barrels use following presets: barrel_s_r2, barrel_s_r4, barrel_s_r8. Each of this presets indicates specific barrel with different radius. They implement barrel_of_destruction.lua script, which is responsible for spawning ribbons for barrels (these, in turn, indicate radius of a spawned barrel), deleting said ribbons when a barrel explodes and, finally, damaging and slowing all actors within its radius.

6. Blinding barrel.

Blinding barrel is a variation of exploding barrel that stores flammable liquid of type C. Not unlike other barrels, it can be used as a weapon and provides an excellent option for making ambushes. Blinding barrels come with different radiuses, ranging from 2 to 8 meters. When exploded, barrel inflicts fire damage equal to 5 health points and forces actor to move in a random direction for a short time

Blinding barrels use following presets: barrel_b_r2, barrel_b_r4, barrel_b_r8. Each of this presets indicates specific barrel with a different radius. They implement beam_of_destruction.lua script, which is responsible for spawning ribbons for barrels (these, in turn, indicate radius of a spawned barrel), deleting said ribbons when a barrel explodes, damaging actors in range and changing their movement direction.

7. Snares.

This object occupies a circular area on the map with radius of 1 or 2 meters. When snares are raised, they deal 2.5 health points per second to all actors in range, immobilizing them in process. When lowered, snares should be considered inactive and, as such, do nothing at all.

Snares use following presets: snares_of_hatred_r1_a, snares_of_hatred_r2_a and snares_of_hatred_r4_a. Each of this presets indicates specific snares with different radius. They implement snare_of_hatred.lua, which is responsible for spawning ribbons for snares, spawning icons for actors affected, said icons and dealing damage and immobilizing actors in range when triggered

8. Teleport.

Teleport is an alien technology instrument that, when entered will warp target to an unknown destination.

Teleport uses teleport preset, implementing teleport.lua which is responsible for warping targets to a different location.

9. Double damage.

Double damage is a power up item that doubles damage dealt by an actor permamently.

Double damage uses double_damage preset, implementing double_damage.lua script which is responsible for increasing actor’s damage and spawning damage buff icon.

10. Healing bandage.

Healing bandage is a pick up item that restores 20 health points to the actor.

Healing badge uses healing_bandage preset, implementing “healing_bandage.lua” script which is responsible for restoring actor's health points.

11. Coordinator.

Coordinator is an invisible object responsible for maintaining progresion of a map, for example: respawning destroyed barrels, spawning pick up items like double damage or healing bandage, destroying deprecated icons etc.

2. arena_b

1. Claymore trap.

A Claymore Trap is a trap that triggers when stepped on, dealing damage to everyone in radius of 2 meters. If not tripped by the end of the effect, the Claymore Trap will deactivate.

Claymore trap uses following presets: claymore_trap_r2, claymore_trap_r1. Each of these indicates specific trap with different radius. They implement claymore_trap.lua script, which is responsible for dealing damage to actor who stepped on trap and disabling trap when its effect time is over.

2. Knockback trap.

A knockback trap is a trap (yup!) that activates when an actor steps into its radius. When activated, the affected target is pushed back 6 meters. Caution: works only on Player. If not tripped by the end of the effect, the knockback trap will deactivate.

Knockback trap use following presets: knockback_trap_r1, knockback_trap_r2. Each of this presets indicates specific trap with a different radius. They implement knockback_trap.lua script, which is responsible for pushing back actors and disabling trap when its effect time is over.

3. Land mine.

A Land Mine is visible trap that triggers when stepped on, damaging all actors in its radius. Land Mine is made to simulate falling damage. Land Mines deal 5 points of damage to all actor affected and immobilize them for 2 seconds.

Land mine uses: land_mine_r2 preset, implementing land_mine.lua script responsible for dealing damage to an actor and immobilizing them for given time.
