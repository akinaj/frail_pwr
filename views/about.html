About FRAIL

FRAIL

FRAIL, as in FRamework for AI Laboratories, is an application designed for testing artificial intelligence of game bosses in a custom environment made for that purpose.

FRAIL was originally made as a project for Artificial Intelligence class in Wroclaw University of Technology by Andrzej Cwenar and Maciej Kurowski, under the name AI SandBox. Then, decision was made to continue expanding it as an finishing project for bachelor of sciences degree. At this point, they were joined by Sebastian Łasisz and Miłosz Mazur. A project's supervisor was dr inż. Paweł Myszkowski.

Today, we aim to continue expanding FRAIL as a complex research tool, to deliver more options, more space, and to provide excellent-working and no less looking content. To boldly go where no AI environment has gone before.

What you get

Our application was originally made for writing you own controllers and testing them in provided environment (like a 'game' of sorts) - to learn how, head to our quick start guide, but no one will stop you from playing around with built-in controllers first! We've included some pretty simple and popular solutions for you to observe, test and - ultimately - to base your own controllers on.

The package you download provides you with 3 basic options:

  • Launch FRAIL and enjoy built-in map scenarios
  • Modify supported maps with our Blender plugin (requires Blender's Foundation Blender) - click here to learn how
  • Write you own AI controller compatibile with FRAIL framework and test it in our environment (requires code compilation)
